About CDSS

Community Day Secondary Schools (CDSS) in Malawi were established to increase access to secondary education for students across the country. These schools play an important role in providing affordable and accessible education to communities, especially in rural areas. MTUWA CDSS is committed to academic excellence, discipline, leadership development, and preparing learners with skills needed for university, employment, and entrepreneurship. The school promotes equal educational opportunities and contributes to national development through quality learning.
